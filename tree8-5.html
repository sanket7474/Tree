Terminalia mantaly

Local Name:- Indian Christmas tree

Small to medium tree, 10-20m height. Crown layered in symmetrical tiers. Leaves small (2-5cm long x 1cm wide), mottled grey-green with irregular creamy-white border, young leaves pinkish-red, margins uneven to crenate, produced in whorls at branch tips. Flowers and fruits not observed in Singapore. Bark smooth, mottled pale grey, with protuding brownish lenticels (streaks or spots).Suitable for parks and along street avenues due to architectural look and ornamental colourful leaves. Fast-growing on good sites. Drought-tolerant when established. May revert to pure green leaves of species form -- prune these branches away when they appear.
